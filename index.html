Summary

I am a Computer Science and Engineering graduate from East West University with a strong interest in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, IoT, and Robotics. My capstone project focused on Explainable AI in Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI), combining AI and ML with neuroscience to improve human-computer interaction. I have worked on over 60 prototype projects and published 14 research papers, including two in Q1 journals. I have practical experience as an Assistant Intern at Rigel Shipping Services, where I helped install lighthouse beacons, and as a Project Manager at the East West University Robotics Club, where I led research-based projects. My skills include Python, C++, SQL, AI, IoT systems, Robotics, Machine Learning, Embedded systems and microcontroller-based automation. I am eager to expand my knowledge and apply technology to solve real-world problems.
